Camera Movement Keyboard Commands

Up Arrow Move forward
Down Arrow Move backward
Left Arrow Move left
Right Arrow Move right
SHIFT + Up Arrow Move up
SHIFT + Down Arrow Move down

 
  Camera Rotation Keyboard Commands

W Rotate up
S Rotate down
A Rotate left
D Rotate right
Q Roll left
E Roll right

 
  Camera Movement Mouse Actions

Hold left button pressed and move mouse up Move forward
Hold left button pressed and move mouse down Move backward
Hold both pressed CTRL key and left button and move mouse left Move left
Hold both pressed CTRL key and left button and move mouse right Move right
Hold both pressed SHIFT key and left button and move mouse up Move up
Hold both pressed SHIFT key and left button and move mouse down Move down

 
  Camera Rotation Mouse Actions

a) Hold both pressed SHIFT key and right button and move mouse up
b) Roll mouse wheel down 		Rotate up
a) Hold both pressed SHIFT key and right button and move mouse down
b) Roll mouse wheel up 		Rotate down
a) Hold right button pressed and move mouse left
b) Hold SHIFT key pressed and roll mouse wheel up 		Rotate left
a) Hold right button pressed and move mouse right
b) Hold SHIFT key pressed and roll mouse wheel down 		Rotate right
a) Hold both pressed CTRL key and right button and move mouse left
b) Hold CTRL key pressed and roll mouse wheel down 		Roll left
a) Hold both pressed CTRL key and right button and move mouse right
b) Hold CTRL key pressed and roll mouse wheel up 		Roll right

 
  Quick Activation of Selected Camera with Keyboard

Keys 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9 and 0 are used to switch camera in the focused view. Totally 10 cameras present in scene.

 
  FOV Change Keyboard Commands for Active Camera

PLUS Increase camera FOV
MINUS Decrease camera FOV

 
  FOV Change Mouse Actions for Active Camera

Hold both pressed CTRL and SHIFT keys and roll mouse wheel.

 
  Other Keyboard Commands

CTRL + A Toggle scene animation
CTRL + Z Toggle planet objects
CTRL + X Toggle camera axes
CTRL + C Toggle cube objects
CTRL + V Toggle text objects
CTRL + B Toggle outer objects
CTRL + N Toggle AVI player window
CTRL + M Toggle mirror window
